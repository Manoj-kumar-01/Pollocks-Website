What is the student-to-teacher ratio?

expand_more

We maintain a strict 12:1 ratio in Early Years and 18:1 in Secondary levels to ensure personalized attention for every student.

Are scholarships available for new students?

expand_more

Yes, we offer Merit Scholarships and Talent-Based Awards for students entering Grade 6 and above. Applications close Nov 15th.

Does the school provide transport?

expand_more

We operate a premium fleet of air-conditioned buses covering all major residential districts with real-time GPS tracking for parents.

How are extracurriculars integrated?

expand_more

Arts, sports, and community service are core pillars. Two hours every afternoon are dedicated to elective clubs and professional coaching.
